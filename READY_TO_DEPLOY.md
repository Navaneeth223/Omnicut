## 💡 HOW TO USE

### Creating Shorts (30 seconds)

1. **Open**: http://localhost:5173/
2. **Import Media**:
   - Click "Media Pool"
   - Click "Import Media"
   - Select 5-10 images
   - Select 1 music file (optional)
3. **Create Shorts**:
   - Click "🎬 AI Shorts" tab
   - Click a template
   - Click images in order
   - Click music (optional)
   - Click "✨ Generate Shorts!"
4. **Export**:
   - Click "📤 Export Video"
   - Choose format (MP4)
   - Download and share!
