# 🎬 AI Shorts Studio - Quick Reference

## Open the App
```
http://localhost:5173/
```
**AI Shorts Studio opens by default!**

---

## 3-Click Workflow

### 1️⃣ Choose Template
Click one:
- 🤖 **AI Image Slideshow** - For AI art (60s, 9:16)
- ⚡ **Quick Montage** - Fast-paced (30s, 9:16)
- ✨ **Smooth Story** - Elegant (60s, 9:16)
- 📱 **Instagram Feed** - Square (60s, 1:1)

### 2️⃣ Select Images
- Click images from Media Pool
- They'll be numbered in order
- Recommended: 5-10 images

### 3️⃣ Add Music (Optional)
- Click an audio file
- Or skip this step

### ✨ Generate!
Click "✨ Generate Shorts!" and done!

---

## What Happens Automatically

✅ **Project settings** updated (resolution, aspect ratio)  
✅ **Images** added to timeline  
✅ **Transitions** applied between clips  
✅ **Effects** applied to all clips  
✅ **Music** synced to video length  
✅ **Ready** to preview and export!

---

## Export Your Video

1. Click "📤 Export Video"
2. Choose:
   - Format: **MP4**
   - Codec: **H.264**
   - Quality: **High**
3. Click "Start Export"
4. Download and share!

## Keyboard Shortcuts

- **Esc** - Go back
- **Enter** - Next step
- **Space** - Preview (after generation)
- **Ctrl+E** - Export

---

## Tips

✅ Use 5-10 images for 60s videos  
✅ Select images in the order you want  
✅ Use high-quality images (1080p+)  
✅ Match music to template style  
✅ Preview before exporting  

---

## Troubleshooting

**No images?** → Import to Media Pool first  
**No music?** → Skip or import audio files  
**Export fails?** → Use H.264 codec  
**Need help?** → See `AI_SHORTS_STUDIO_GUIDE.md`

## Example: Create AI Art Shorts

1. Import 8 AI images + 1 music track
2. Click "🤖 AI Image Slideshow"
3. Click all 8 images
4. Click music track
5. Click "Generate!"
6. Click "Export"
7. Share on TikTok!

**Total time: 30 seconds** ⚡

## Switch to Manual Editing

1. Click "Edit" tab at top

3. Adjust as needed
4. Export when ready
