## 🎯 How to Use

### AI Shorts Studio (Default View)

1. **Open the app**: http://localhost:5173/
2. **Import media**: Click Media Pool → Import images and music
3. **Create shorts**:
   - Click a template (e.g., "🤖 AI Image Slideshow")
   - Click 5-10 images
   - Click music track (optional)
   - Click "✨ Generate Shorts!"
4. **Export**: Click "📤 Export Video"
5. **Share**: Download and post to TikTok/Reels/Shorts!

**Time**: 30 seconds  
**Clicks**: 3  
**Result**: Professional video

### Traditional Editor

1. Click "Edit" tab at top
2. Import media to Media Pool
3. Drag clips to timeline
4. Apply effects and transitions
5. Export video

**Time**: 10-30 minutes  
**Skill**: Basic editing knowledge  
**Result**: Custom edited video
