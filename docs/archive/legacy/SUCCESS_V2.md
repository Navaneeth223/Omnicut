## 🎓 Quick Tutorial

### Your First Shorts (30 seconds)

1. **Open the app** (already running!)
2. **Import media**:
   - Click Media Pool → Import
   - Select 5-10 images
   - Select 1 music track
3. **Create shorts**:
   - Click "🤖 AI Image Slideshow"
   - Click "Next"
   - Click each image
   - Click "Next"
   - Click music track
   - Click "✨ Generate Shorts!"

   - Download!
