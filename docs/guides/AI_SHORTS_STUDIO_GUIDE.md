## How to Use (3-Click Workflow)

### Step 1: Choose a Template (1 click)
Click on a pre-designed template that matches your content style:

#### 🤖 AI Image Slideshow
- **Best for**: AI-generated images, art, creative content
- **Duration**: 60 seconds
- **Format**: 9:16 (Vertical - TikTok/Reels)
- **Transitions**: Cross dissolve, zoom in/out
- **Effects**: Brightness, vignette
- **Music**: Upbeat

#### ⚡ Quick Montage
- **Best for**: Fast-paced content, highlights
- **Duration**: 30 seconds
- **Format**: 9:16 (Vertical)
- **Transitions**: Wipe, push
- **Effects**: Sharpen, film grain
- **Music**: Energetic

#### ✨ Smooth Story
- **Best for**: Storytelling, emotional content
- **Duration**: 60 seconds
- **Format**: 9:16 (Vertical)
- **Transitions**: Fade, cross dissolve
- **Effects**: Brightness, hue/saturation
- **Music**: Ambient

#### 📱 Instagram Feed
- **Best for**: Instagram posts
- **Duration**: 60 seconds
- **Format**: 1:1 (Square)
- **Transitions**: Cross dissolve, dip to black
- **Effects**: Vignette, film grain
- **Music**: Chill

### Step 2: Select Images (1 click per image)
- Click on images from your media pool
- Images will be numbered in order
- Recommended: 5-10 images for 60s video
- Each image gets equal screen time
- Click again to deselect

**Pro Tip**: The order you select images is the order they'll appear in your video!

### Step 3: Add Music (1 click - Optional)
- Click on an audio file from your media pool
- Music will be automatically trimmed to video length
- Skip this step if you don't want music

### Step 4: Generate! (1 click)
Click "✨ Generate Shorts!" and watch the magic happen:
- ✅ Project settings updated (resolution, aspect ratio)
- ✅ Images added to timeline
- ✅ Transitions applied between clips
- ✅ Effects applied to all clips
- ✅ Music added and synced
- ✅ Ready to preview and export!

## Workflow Example

### Creating an AI Art Slideshow

**Scenario**: You have 8 AI-generated images and want to create a 60-second TikTok video.

**Steps**:
1. **Import Images**
   - Go to Media Pool
   - Import your 8 AI images
   - Import a background music track

2. **Open AI Shorts Studio**
   - Click "🎬 AI Shorts" tab at the top
   - You'll see the 3-step wizard

3. **Step 1: Choose Template**
   - Click "🤖 AI Image Slideshow"
   - Click "Next: Select Images →"

4. **Step 2: Select Images**
   - Click each of your 8 images in order
   - They'll be numbered 1-8
   - Click "Next: Add Music →"

5. **Step 3: Add Music**
   - Click your music track
   - Click "✨ Generate Shorts!"

6. **Done!**
   - Video is created on timeline
   - Each image shows for 7.5 seconds
   - Smooth transitions between images
   - Effects applied automatically
   - Music synced perfectly

7. **Export**
   - Click "📤 Export Video"
   - Choose MP4 format
   - Download and share!

**Total Time**: ~30 seconds! 🎉

## Tips & Tricks

### Image Selection
- ✅ **Use similar styles**: Images with similar colors/themes work best
- ✅ **High quality**: Use high-resolution images (1080p+)
- ✅ **Consistent aspect**: Images with similar aspect ratios look better
- ✅ **Order matters**: Select images in the story order you want

### Music Selection
- ✅ **Match mood**: Choose music that matches your template style
- ✅ **Right length**: Music longer than video duration works best
- ✅ **No copyright**: Use royalty-free music for social media
- ✅ **Upbeat for AI**: Energetic music works great with AI art

### Template Selection
- ✅ **AI Art**: Use "AI Image Slideshow" for generated images
- ✅ **Fast Content**: Use "Quick Montage" for 30s videos
- ✅ **Storytelling**: Use "Smooth Story" for emotional content
- ✅ **Instagram**: Use "Instagram Feed" for square posts

### After Generation
- ✅ **Preview first**: Watch the video before exporting
- ✅ **Adjust if needed**: Switch to "Edit" tab to fine-tune
- ✅ **Add text**: Add titles or captions manually if needed
- ✅ **Export settings**: Use H.264 codec for best compatibility

## Troubleshooting

### "No images imported"
**Solution**: Import images to Media Pool first
1. Go to Media Pool (left panel)
2. Click "Import Media"
3. Select your images
4. Return to AI Shorts Studio

### "No audio files imported"
**Solution**: This is optional! You can:
1. Skip music and generate without it
2. Or import audio files to Media Pool first

### Video looks wrong
**Solution**: Check your images
- Use high-quality images (1080p+)
- Ensure images are not corrupted
- Try different images

### Export fails
**Solution**: 
1. Wait for FFmpeg to download (first time only)
2. Try H.264 codec (most compatible)
3. Check browser console for errors
