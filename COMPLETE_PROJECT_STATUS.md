## 🎨 Design System

### Colors
- Background: 4 levels (app, panel, surface, elevated)
- Text: 4 levels (primary, secondary, tertiary, disabled)
- Accent: Gradient (indigo → cyan)
- Semantic: Success, warning, error, info

### Typography
- Font: Outfit (UI), JetBrains Mono (code)
- Sizes: 10px - 48px (responsive)
- Weights: 400, 500, 600, 700
- Letter spacing: 0.02em - 0.05em

### Spacing
- Scale: 4px base (--sp-1 to --sp-10)
- Responsive: Larger on bigger screens

### Animations
- Easing: Spring, ease-out, ease-in-out
- Duration: Fast (120ms), normal (220ms), slow (380ms)
- Reduced motion support
